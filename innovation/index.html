Data Innovation Labs

By applying iterative and experimental principles used by successful entrepreneurs, organizations can discover new ways of promoting data innovation among their workforce. The Data Innovation Lab program is designed to extract great data application ideas from employees through team competition, professional recognition and monetary prizes. The program operates similar to a start-up incubator where ideas are turned into working solutions during a period of 30-45 days. Ideas are fleshed out through continuous experimentation and validation. Similar to ABCâ€™s Shark Tank, the end goal of the program is to present vetted proof-of-concept solutions to the executive management team for selection and funding approval. Data SWAT Team provides the leadership and technical expertise required to ensure the program stays on track and meets its stated goals. The program can be customized to fit any organizational structure.
